Breakfasts

Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: Miras
Ingredients Quantity
kefir or yogurt 1 cup
egg 1
sugar 1 tbsp
baking soda 1 tbs
flour 1.5 cups
salt pinch

Steps: Mix kefir, egg, sugar, and salt. Add baking soda and flour. Fry pancakes on a hot pan until golden brown.

Cheese Omelette

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: Miras
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
cheese 50g
milk 2 tbsp
salt pinch

Steps: Whisk eggs with milk and salt. Add grated cheese. Cook under a lid for 5–7 minutes.

Desserts

Homemade Cookies

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: Miras
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 100 g
egg 1
sugar 100 g
flour 250 g

Steps: Mix all ingredients, shape cookies, and bake for 20 minutes at 180°C.

Hot Chocolate

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: Miras
Ingredients Quantity
milk 1 cup
cocoa powder 2 tsp
sugar optional

Steps: Heat milk, add cocoa and sugar, stir well.
