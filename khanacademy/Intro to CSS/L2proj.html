Basic info

South Korea, officially known as the Republic of Korea, is a country located in East Asia on the southern part of the Korean Peninsula. It borders North Korea to the north and is surrounded by the Yellow Sea and the Sea of Japan. The capital city, Seoul, is one of the largest and most modern cities in the world.
South Korea is a democratic republic with a stable political system. Since the late 20th century, the country has developed strong democratic institutions and guarantees civil rights and freedoms. The government plays an important role in supporting innovation, education, and economic growth.
